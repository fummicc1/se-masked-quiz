Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 13, 2025

1. Introduction

SE Masked Quiz ("the App") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

2. Information Collection

The App collects the following information:

  • Learning Data: Quiz progress, scores, answer history, etc.

The App does not collect personally identifiable information (name, email address, phone number, etc.).

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • To save learning progress and restore it on next use
  • To enhance user experience

4. Data Storage

All learning data is stored locally on your device. Data is never transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Sharing

The App does not share user personal information or learning data with third parties.

6. Fetching Swift Evolution Proposals

The App requires an internet connection to fetch Swift Evolution proposal content. Proposal data is retrieved from the official Swift Evolution repository. No personally identifiable information is transmitted during this communication.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Significant changes will be notified within the App or on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: fumiya.tennis1@gmail.com

10. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App will delete all learning data stored on your device.
