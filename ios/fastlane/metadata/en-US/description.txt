Learn Swift's Evolution Through Interactive Quizzes

Swift Evolution Quiz is an educational app that helps you master Swift Evolution proposals through interactive fill-in-the-blank questions. Instead of just reading proposals, actively test your understanding and retain knowledge more effectively.


Key Features

◆ Interactive Learning Experience
Important keywords in proposals are masked. Tap them to reveal 4-choice quizzes. Test your understanding and learn terminology in context.

◆ Comprehensive Coverage
Access all official Swift Evolution proposals. From the latest features to historical changes, understand Swift's complete evolution.

◆ Progress Tracking
Track scores for each proposal with percentage-based results. See at a glance which proposals you've mastered and which need review.

◆ Learn at Your Pace
Choose any proposal and start learning. Your progress auto-saves, so you can resume anytime.

◆ Reset Function
Want to test your knowledge again? Reset any proposal's quiz to retake it.
